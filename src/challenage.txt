Section 7 - The Challenge

 

You are a Data Analyst assisting a veterinarian clinic make sense of their
data. Their data is dispersed across multiple csv files and they need you to
first upload all of them to database and then perform the following
analytics:

Extract information on pets names and owner names side-by-side

Find out which pets from this clinic had procedures performed

Match up all procedures performed to their descriptions

Same as above but only or pets from the clinic in question

Extract a table of individual costs (procedure prices) incurred by owners
of pets from the clinic in question (this table should have owner and
procedure price side-by-side)
